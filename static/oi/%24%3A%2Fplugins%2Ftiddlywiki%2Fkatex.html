KaTeX: KaTeX library for mathematical typography 

$:/plugins/tiddlywiki/katex

5.1.23

This is a TiddlyWiki plugin for mathematical and chemical typesetting based on KaTeX from Khan Academy (v0.10.2) and mhchem through a Katex extension.

It is completely self-contained, and doesn't need an Internet connection in order to work. It works both in the browser and under Node.js.
