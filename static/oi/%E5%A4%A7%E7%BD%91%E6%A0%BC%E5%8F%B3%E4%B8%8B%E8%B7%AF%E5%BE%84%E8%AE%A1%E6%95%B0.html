fi=Num[(1,1)→(ri,ci)]−∑jfj⋅Num[(rj,cj)→(ri,ci)] f_i = Num[(1,1)\to(r_i,c_i)] - \sum_j f_j\cdot Num[(r_j,c_j)\to(r_i,c_i)] fi​=Num[(1,1)→(ri​,ci​)]−j∑​fj​⋅Num[(rj​,cj​)→(ri​,ci​)]
